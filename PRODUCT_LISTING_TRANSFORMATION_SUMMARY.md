## 🎯 What Your Users Can Now Do

### **Scenario 1: "I want to find affordable makhana in stock quickly"**

2. **Sort:** Price Low to High
3. **Result:** See available affordable options instantly ✓

### **Scenario 2: "I want to compare products side-by-side"**
1. **Click View Toggle:** Switch to List View
2. **Benefit:** See multiple products details on one screen
3. **Result:** Quick comparison of name, price, rating ✓

### **Scenario 3: "I want to preview a product before buying"**
1. **Hover over product** → Click "Quick View"
2. **Modal shows:** Full images, specs, price, reviews, stock
3. **Action:** Add to cart directly from modal
4. **Result:** Fast, frictionless shopping ✓

### **Scenario 4: "Show me your best-selling premium products"**
